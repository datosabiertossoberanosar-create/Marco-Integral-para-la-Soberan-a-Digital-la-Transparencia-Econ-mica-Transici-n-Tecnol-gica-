---

## Description

This repository contains the final and complete version of the legislative package entitled:

**“Marco Integral para la Soberanía Digital, la Transparencia Económica y la Transición Tecnológica Sostenible”**

The package is composed of eight (8) interconnected draft laws designed as a comprehensive State-level legal architecture addressing:

- digital sovereignty and open data governance  
- economic transparency and beneficial ownership  
- ethical tokenization and asset traceability  
- disclosure of strategic external assets  
- responsible investment incentives and technological rent  
- inclusive digital transition and technological basic income  
- environmental sustainability of technological infrastructure  
- cybersecurity and digital identity protection  

The framework is intended for legislative evaluation, institutional dialogue and international cooperation.
